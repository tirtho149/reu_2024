Research Experience Overview

During Summer 2024, I participated in the Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) program as a Research Intern at the Translational AI Center (TrAC) at Iowa State University.

Program Components:

  • Intensive "Deep Dive Into AI Boot Camp" (June 4-7, 2024)
  • Weekly REU seminars featuring speakers from academia
  • Visited TrAC industry partners John Deere and Corteva
  • Collaborative research with TrAC faculty and graduate students
  • Development of an AI-driven sales pitch and customer insight generator utilizing Large Language Models
  • Final presentation at the university-wide REU Research Symposium

This program provided extensive experience in machine learning methodologies, data analysis techniques, and AI applications in business contexts, while fostering professional connections with leading researchers in the field of translational artificial intelligence.

Research Project

AI-driven Sales Pitch and Customer Insight Generator

This research developed an AI system leveraging Large Language Models and Big Five personality traits to enhance sales outcomes, customer satisfaction, and real-time decision support for businesses.

Research Objectives:

  • Create personalized sales pitches based on customer personality profiles
  • Develop real-time feedback mechanisms for sales interactions
  • Analyze effectiveness of tailored communication strategies

Faculty Mentors: Arbab Arshad, Souradeep Chattopadhyay, Priyanka Jayashankar, Soumik Sarkar
